Solution for question 1:

SELECT Extract(Month from b.event_date) as month ,
count(DISTINCT b.user_id) as monthly_active_users 
FROM user_actions a
JOIN user_actions b
on a.user_id=b.user_id
AND b.event_date between '07/01/2022 12:00:00' and '07/31/2022 11:59:59'
AND a.event_date between '06/01/2022 12:00:00' and '06/30/2022 11:59:59'
group by month;


Solution for question 2:

SELECT count(*) as payment_count 
FROM transactions t1 
join TRANSACTIONs t2 
on t1.transaction_id < t2.transaction_id
AND t1.merchant_id = t2.merchant_id 
-- AND t1.credit_card_id= t2.credit_card_id
AND t1.amount = t2.amount
And EXTRACT(epoch from (t2.TRANSACTION_timestamp -t1.TRANSACTION_timestamp))<=600
